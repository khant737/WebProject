1. Molecular biology is the branch of biology.

2. Molecular biology seeks to understand the molecular basis of biological activity in and between cells.

3. The study of chemical, physical structures, and functions of biological micromolecules is known as molecular biology.

4. Biomolecular synthesis, modification, mechanisms, and interactions are molecular basis of biological activity.

5. Molecular biology is the study of biology at molecular level.

6. The two main classes of nucleus are deoxyribonucleic acid and ribonucleic acid.

7. Nucleic acids are the main information carrying molecule of the cell.

8. Nucleic acids are direct the process of photosynthesis.

9. RNA is the master blueprint for life and constitutes the genetic materials in all free-living organisms.

10. RNA is the genetic material of certain viruses, but it is also found in all living cells.
